Delhi


Delhi, India's bustling capital, is a mesmerizing blend of ancient history and modern vibrancy. From majestic Mughal monuments to chaotic street markets and diverse cuisine, it offers an unforgettable sensory overload for any traveler seeking culture, food, and adventure in one dynamic city.

  1. Explore the iconic Red Fort, a UNESCO site built by Shah Jahan.
  2. Marvel at the symmetrical beauty of Humayun's Tomb, the precursor to the Taj Mahal.
  3. Wander through the spiritual serenity of Lotus Temple, shaped like a blooming lotus flower.
  4. Admire India Gate and the nearby Rashtrapati Bhavan, the president's grand residence.
  5. Discover ancient wonders at Qutub Minar, the world's tallest brick minaret.
  • Savor the spicy kick of butter chicken with fluffy naan.
  • Indulge in crispy, stuffed samosas from street vendors.
  • Taste the creamy delight of kulfi, traditional Indian ice cream.
  • Try tangy chaat snacks loaded with chutneys and yogurt.
  • Enjoy aromatic biryani rice layered with spiced meat or veggies.
