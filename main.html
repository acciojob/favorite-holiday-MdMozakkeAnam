Delhi, India


Delhi, the bustling capital city of India, is a mesmerizing tapestry woven with threads of history, culture, and modernity. From the ancient Mughal-era monuments to the modern infrastructure, this incredible city seamlessly blends the old with the new. Founded as Indraprastha over 5,000 years ago, Delhi has been witness to the rise and fall of empires, each leaving their indelible mark on the city's architecture, cuisine, and traditions. The city is divided into two distinct parts: Old Delhi, with its narrow lanes, bustling bazaars, and magnificent Mughal-era structures, and New Delhi, with its wide boulevards, government buildings, and colonial architecture. With its rich heritage, vibrant street life, incredible food scene, and warm hospitality, Delhi offers travelers an unforgettable journey through India's past and present.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Red Fort (Lal Qila) – Explore this magnificent UNESCO World Heritage site built by Emperor Shah Jahan, featuring stunning red sandstone walls and intricate Mughal architecture.
  2. Qutub Minar – Marvel at the world's tallest brick minaret standing at 73 meters, built in the 12th century with five distinct stories featuring intricate carvings.
  3. India Gate – Pay homage at this grandiose war memorial built in 1931, a 42-meter high arch that honors the soldiers of the Indian Army.
  4. Jama Masjid – Visit one of India's largest mosques built by Shah Jahan, featuring a magnificent courtyard that can hold over 25,000 worshippers.
  5. Humayun's Tomb – Discover this beautiful UNESCO World Heritage garden tomb that inspired the Taj Mahal, featuring symmetrical gardens and Persian-style architecture.

Must-Try Foods

  • Chole Bhature – Indulge in Delhi's beloved spicy chickpea curry served with fluffy deep-fried bread, a breakfast staple.
  • Paranthas – Taste the flaky, buttery, and perfectly stuffed flatbreads at Paranthe Wali Gali, offering dozens of traditional fillings.
  • Kebabs – Experience the melt-in-your-mouth tandoori kebabs including seekh kebabs and galouti kebabs, grilled to perfection.
  • Street Chaat – Savor the tangy, spicy, and crunchy street food delights including pani puri, aloo tikki, and papri chaat.
  • Mughlai Cuisine – Delight in rich curries, biryanis, and kormas that reflect Delhi's Mughal heritage with aromatic spices.
